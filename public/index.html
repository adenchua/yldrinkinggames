Let's Get Drunk

This card-game will make you drunk! Compete, vote and screw your friends over with 100 dynamic cards, no two games are the same. It is super easy to play, perfect for your next party, pregame or nights in.

Number of players: 2-8

Ages: 21+

How To Play Play Now
How to play: Let's Get Drunk
The phone is passed around in a clockwise direction. During each turn, a player clicks the button "Next Card" and reads the content of the card out loud. Follow the instructions on the card to determine who drinks that round.

Let the drinking and hilarity ensue!
Drunken Artist

In this version of Pictionary, being bad at drawing will get you drunk.

Number of players: 4-8

Ages: 21+

How To Play Play Now
How to play: Drunken Artist
Players take turns being the client and being the artist. At the beginning of each round, the client will whisper a word to the artist and start the clock. As the artist draws the word, the rest of the players shout out what they think the picture is supposed to be.

After someone guesses correctly, the artist stops the timer. The artist have to take a drink for every 20 seconds that went by. If 2 minutes is up and none guesses correctly, the artist must take a shot.
